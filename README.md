## Monthly Sales Forecasting Dashboard

## Project Problem Statement
The company lacked a clear view of monthly sales performance and future growth trends, making it difficult to plan inventory, allocate resources, and design effective marketing strategies.

## Tools Used
- Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)
- Excel (analysis, forecasting, dashboard design)

## Dataset
- Source: superstore sales data from kaggle

## Business Questions 
1. What is the monthly sales trend over time?
2. Is there any seasonality pattern in sales performance?
3. What will be the expected sales in the next 6–12 months?

## Key Insights
- Total sales reached **$762,786.80** with strong **36% MOM growth**.  
- Sales trend shows a sharp increase toward year‑end, highlighting seasonal demand.
- Forecast shows that future sales will likely follow the same pattern as past data.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts during year-end months where demand is highest.
- Plan inventory in advance for peak seasons to avoid stock shortages.
- Use forecasting results to support monthly sales planning and budgeting.

## Business Impact
- Improved demand forecasting and sales planning accuracy
- Better inventory management during high and low seasons
- Increased ability to make data-driven business decisions
